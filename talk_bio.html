Bryan Russell is a Senior Research Scientist at Adobe in San Francisco, CA and leads the Video Understanding group. He has co-authored over 45 publications and jointly received the Helmholtz “test-of-time” prize at ICCV 2017 for work on “Discovering Objects and their Location in Images”, which appeared at ICCV 2005. He has collaborated on several tech transfers to Adobe’s products, including Media Intelligence in Premiere Pro, camera controls in Adobe Firefly Video Model, video shot type filtering in Stock, automatic ground plane estimation, and automatic portrait segmentation.
